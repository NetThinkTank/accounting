Accounting Combinator

Values

Enter the variables in the form name: value, separated by commas or new lines.
For example, starting_inventory: 10000, ending_inventory: 5000.
Try to group related variables together for readability.

Choices

Enter the answer choices separated by commas or new lines. Don't use commas within numbers.
For example, 5000, 15000, 100000.



Results

Be aware of possible groupings with parentheses.

For example:
ending_inventory - starting_inventory or
- starting_inventory + ending_inventory
might make more sense as:
- (starting_inventory - ending_inventory).
